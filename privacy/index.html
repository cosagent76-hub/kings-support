Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026. This page explains how Kings handles data in the iOS app.

Summary

  • Tracking: No.
  • Kings does not use ads.
  • Kings does not use third-party analytics tools.
  • Kings does not use third-party crash tools.
  • Kings does not track users across apps or websites.
  • Kings does not sell user data.
  • SCIM Ventures does not run a separate backend server for Kings.
  • Player photos are intended to stay local on your device.
  • Optional scorebook sharing uses Apple's iCloud and CloudKit systems.

Data Stored by Kings

Kings stores the score data you enter in the app. This may include player names, game results, hand scores, game history, and player stats.

Kings uses this data to keep score, show history, and show stats.

If you add player photos, those photos are meant to stay on your device.

iCloud and CloudKit

Kings may use Apple's iCloud and CloudKit for family scorebook sharing. This is optional.

If you turn on iCloud sharing, scorebook data may be stored in or shared through Apple's iCloud service. That sharing uses your Apple account.

SCIM Ventures does not run a separate account system or backend server for Kings.

Data and iCloud FAQ

What stays on my device?

In-progress games and player photos are meant to stay on your device.

What can use iCloud?

If you turn on sharing, completed scorebook history may use Apple's iCloud and CloudKit service.

Does SCIM Ventures sell or share my data?

No. SCIM Ventures does not sell user data or share it for ads.

Does Kings use tracking?

No. Kings does not track you across apps or websites.

Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

Kings does not include ad tools, third-party analytics tools, or third-party tracking tools.

Kings does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Support Requests

If you ask for help through the public GitHub support form, what you type may be public on GitHub.

The support form is optional. It is not part of the Kings app, and it is not used for app data collection.

Do not include private personal details in public support requests.

Children's Privacy

Kings is a scorekeeper for a family card game. It is not made to collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may change when Kings changes. The date at the top of this page will show the latest update.

Contact

For help, visit the Kings Support page. GitHub support requests are public, so do not include private details.
